MY FAVOURITE TEMPLE

Vinayaka Temple or Sri Varasidhi Vinayaka Swamy Temple is a Hindu temple of Ganesha. It is located at Kanipakam in Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, India.

MY FAVOURITE SWEET

Gulab jamun is a sweet confectionery or dessert, originating in Persia and then later introduced to the Indian subcontinent. It is popular in Pakistan, India, as well as Myanmar.

MY FAVOURITE HERO

Pawan Kalyan (born Konidela Kalyan Babu; 2 September 1968 or 1971[n 1]) is an Indian politician, actor, filmmaker, and philanthropist. He works in Telugu cinema and is the recipient of a Filmfare Award, a SIIMA Award, a CineMAA Award and a Santosham Film Award.He is also the founder of Jana Sena Party.

MY FAVOURITE ACTRESS

Sweety Shetty (born 7 November 1981), known by her stage name Anushka Shetty, is an Indian actress and model who predominantly works in Telugu and Tamil films.[3] She is the recipient of three Filmfare Awards South, two Nandi Awards, two SIIMA Awards and one Tamil Nadu State Film Award. Having appeared in 47 films, she is one of the highest-paid South Indian actresses[4][5] and is popularly referred as the "Superstar" of South Indian cinema.

MY FAVOURITE ACTOR

Ghanta Naveen Babu (born 24 February 1984), known professionally by his screen name Nani, is an Indian actor, producer, and television presenter primarily known for his work in Telugu cinema. He is the recipient of two Nandi Awards and two Filmfare Awards, apart from three Filmfare Best Actor nominations.
